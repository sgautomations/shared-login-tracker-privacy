Privacy Policy for Shared Login Tracker

Last updated: 07/28/2025

Overview

The Shared Login Tracker Chrome extension is designed exclusively for Solar Gain Inc employees to prevent login conflicts when sharing business application credentials. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how it's used.

Data We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: We collect your Chrome-signed-in email address to verify you are a Solar Gain Inc employee (@solargaininc.com domain) and to identify you in our conflict tracking system.

Usage Information

  • Website URLs: We monitor the URLs of websites you visit to detect potential login conflicts with other Solar Gain Inc employees.
  • Tab Activity: We track when you switch tabs or navigate to new pages to provide real-time conflict detection.

How We Use Your Data

  • Verify employee authorization (Solar Gain Inc domain validation)
  • Track which employee is currently using shared credentials
  • Provide conflict warnings to prevent login issues
  • Display personalized alerts and status information

Data Storage and Sharing

  • Local Storage: Your email address is stored locally in the Chrome extension
  • Company Systems: Data is sent to Solar Gain Inc's Google Apps Script for conflict detection
  • No Third Parties: We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with any third parties
  • Company Use Only: Data is used exclusively for the stated purpose of preventing login conflicts

Data Security

Your data is transmitted securely to Solar Gain Inc's Google Apps Script using HTTPS encryption. Access is restricted to authorized Solar Gain Inc employees only.

Data Retention

Email addresses are stored locally until you uninstall the extension. URL tracking data is used for real-time conflict detection and is not permanently stored beyond active session monitoring.

Your Rights

As a Solar Gain Inc employee, you can:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time to stop data collection
  • Contact Solar Gain Inc IT support for questions about your data

Changes to This Policy

Solar Gain Inc may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or the Shared Login Tracker extension, please contact Solar Gain Inc IT support.

Compliance

This extension and privacy policy comply with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • Google Chrome Extension requirements
  • Solar Gain Inc internal data handling policies
